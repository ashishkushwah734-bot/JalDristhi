Our Mission

Jal Drishti empowers communities to protect local water bodies. Our platform enables citizens to instantly report water contamination, leakage, or resource mismanagement to localized authorities.

Key Features

  • Media Reporting: Snap real-time photos and geolocations of water issues directly to our encrypted servers.
  • AI pH Detection: Leverage AI processing to detect visual water acidity anomalies on-site.
  • Live Map: View interactive heatmap vectors of reported issues in your active vicinity.
  • Critical Alerts: Receive instant push notifications about severe water hazards globally.
